<p><strong>The Broadcast Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite Video Monitoring</li><li>S-Video Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Broadcast Monitoring Systems encompass various market types, including Composite Video Monitoring and S-Video Monitoring. Composite Video Monitoring captures and analyzes signals combining video and audio into a single channel, allowing for straightforward surveillance of broadcast quality. In contrast, S-Video Monitoring separates luminance and chrominance, resulting in superior video clarity and detail, making it ideal for high-quality video production environments. Both types cater to different broadcast standards, ensuring compliance and enhancing overall monitoring efficiency in media operations.</p></p>
