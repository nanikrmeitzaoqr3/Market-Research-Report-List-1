<p><strong>The Fluorinated Plasma Surface Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pressure Plasma</li><li>Atmospheric Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorinated plasma surface treatment encompasses two primary market types: low-pressure plasma and atmospheric plasma. Low-pressure plasma treatment operates in a controlled vacuum environment, allowing for uniform processing of complex shapes and achieving deep surface modifications with high precision. In contrast, atmospheric plasma treatment functions at ambient pressure, effectively treating surfaces in real-time and offering high-speed processing. Both techniques enhance surface properties such as adhesion, wettability, and chemical resistance, making them valuable in industries like electronics, automotive, and biomedical applications.</p></p>
